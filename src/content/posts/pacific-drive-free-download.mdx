## The plot of the game

The game takes place in the near future on the northwest coast of the USA. As a result of a mysterious cataclysm, the area has been destroyed and filled with dangerous anomalies, forcing most people to leave.

The player takes on the role of a daredevil who uses his car to reach the so-called Olympic Exclusion Zone, located in the heart of these transformed lands, believing that there he will finally find out what caused the cataclysm.

## Game mechanics

Pacific Drive is a survival-action game in which events are viewed from a first-person perspective. We spend most of the game in a car, driving through dangerous terrain. We are threatened by anomalies and various monsters, as well as the usual but equally dangerous forces of nature.

The player can also get out of the car. This is necessary in situations where it is necessary to pick up items or repair damage to the machine, for example. The car itself can be extensively modified, its parts can be upgraded, more and better "armour" and gadgets can be added, and the vehicle can be adapted to our visual preferences.

All of this is enriched by light roguelike elements. The game takes the form of dangerous but relatively short expeditions, between which we return to the garage that serves as our base. On each trip, many elements of the world, including resources, monsters and anomalies, are randomly generated to provide us with new challenges.
