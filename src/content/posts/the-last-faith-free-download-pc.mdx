## The plot of the game

Eric, who suffers from amnesia, takes matters into his own hands and, while searching for answers to questions about an ancient prophecy, becomes embroiled in an intrigue of much greater magnitude. ## Game Mechanics

The Last Faith is a side-scrolling action game. Throughout the game, we traverse vast and varied locations, including mighty citadels, palaces and temples, or the trembling underground. All of these places make up a large map, creating a kind of elaborate labyrinth, and although we can roam freely through them, we can only gain access to many areas after acquiring certain equipment or skills.

The exploration is enriched by environmental puzzles and encounters with all kinds of monsters - from walking to flying to crawling.
