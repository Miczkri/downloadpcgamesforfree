## Game Mechanics

In Ara: History Untold we see the action from a bird's eye view. During the game, we stand at the head of a chosen nation and our task is to lead it through successive eras towards a bright future. It is up to us whether we choose to impersonate one of the historical figures, such as Safona, Nefertiti or George Washington, or create our own leader from scratch.

As the game progresses, we will need to discover and conquer new territories and acquire valuable resources. The latter will be used to build a variety of structures that will provide us with skills and strategic bonuses, as well as constant technological development that will give us access to new goods, resources and improvements. We should also promote culture and the arts, and build relationships with neighbours with whom we can trade or form alliances. And if diplomacy fails, we can raise a powerful army and send it into battle. But as we think globally, we should not forget the individual, for every citizen here has a unique personality and history, shaped in part by our actions.
