## Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 game mechanics

In Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024, we will once again have the opportunity to explore the entire globe at the controls of civilian aircraft, starting and ending our journey at one of tens of thousands of airports located in different corners of the world. Thanks to the different levels of difficulty, the simulator is able to challenge veterans and at the same time provide enjoyable entertainment for amateurs.

In addition to moving freely between points on the map, we participate in activities developed in cooperation with emergency services. These activities realistically reproduce real-life situations, allowing us to, for example, extinguish fires from the air, transport the wounded, or take over people who are stuck in hard-to-reach places (such as mountains).
