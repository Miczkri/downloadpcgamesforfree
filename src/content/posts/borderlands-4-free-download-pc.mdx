## Borderlands 4 offers a whole new adventure!

Kairos is a brand new planet in the Borderlands universe that has been designed to be more challenging and unpredictable than the well-known Pandora. The developers describe it as the most dangerous place in the galaxy to emphasise both the level of challenge and the diversity of enemies.

The planet is also much more diverse than Pandora. We will see ruined cities that hide the secrets of long-forgotten civilisations and vast wastelands in Borderlands 4. Kairos is also supposed to be more interactive: we will discover hidden paths and elements of the environment that can be used in combat, as well as mysterious objects related to the planet's history. Uncovering these secrets will be one of the main storylines, encouraging players to explore every corner.

The plot is said to be linked to the ending of the previous instalment, in which Lilith, the leader of the Crimson Raiders and a powerful Siren, sacrifices herself to save Pandora from destruction. After defeating Tyreen Calypso, Lilith regains her powers and uses them to stop the moon Elpis from colliding with Pandora. The moon is moved into orbit around Kairos, causing chaos that threatens the rule of the local dictator, the Timekeeper. He will be the new antagonist that the heroes will face.
