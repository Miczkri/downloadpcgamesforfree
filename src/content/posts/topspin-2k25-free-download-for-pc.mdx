## Game Mechanics

With official licences, TopSpin 2K25 gives us the opportunity to take part in four Grand Slam tournaments: Wimbledon, Roland-Garros, US Open and Australian Open. As we fight for the title of Grand Slam Champion, we will compete against real players, including the biggest names in the sport, such as Roger Federer and Serena Williams, as well as Iga Swiatek (who is featured on the cover of the Deluxe Edition), Carlos Alcaraz, Frances Tiafoe and even Andre Agassi.

The title allows us to play as one of the stars or create our own player using the extensive MyPLAYER wizard. We can choose not only the gender and appearance of our character, but also the clothing, accessories and equipment from companies such as adidas, HEAD, Lacoste, New Balance, Nike and Wilson.

## Game Modes

TopSpin 2K25 can be played either solo or in multiplayer mode (over the Internet or locally).

In addition to the MyCAREER mode, which takes you from beginner to tennis star, the World Tour and 2K Tour modules are also available. The game also features the TopSpin Academy, a special mode in which the legendary John McEnroe helps us to improve our skills through various exercises and challenges.
