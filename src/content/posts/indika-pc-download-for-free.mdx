## The plot of the game

Indika takes us on a journey to an alternative version of 19th century Russia, where religious visions mix with reality. The main character is the titular Indika, a young nun who decides to leave the cold convent walls and embark on a journey to discover herself. Along the way, the protagonist discovers the truth behind religion and authority, as well as the dualistic nature of good and evil. At the same time, Indika is not condemned to a lonely journey, as she is accompanied by the devil himself.

Although all this sounds too serious, the story told in the game can be described as a tragicomedy.
