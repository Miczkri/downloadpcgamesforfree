## The plot of the game

The main character of Unknown 9: Awakening is Haroona, played by Anya Chalotra, known for her role as Yennefer in the Witcher series on the Netflix platform. Under the guidance of a mentor, the protagonist learns to master her supernatural abilities to control the unseen and gains access to a hidden dimension known as the Fault, whose secrets she seeks to explore. Haroona's abilities lead to her being kidnapped by the so-called Dominators, led by their founder, Vincent Lichter, who plan to use her to change the course of human history.

## Nine key books

Key to the world of Unknown 9 are nine books that contain the accumulated wisdom of all the civilisations that have ever walked the Earth, each of which reached an incredibly high level of development before self-destructing. To ensure that their knowledge was not lost, it was decided to write it down. Instead, the books were to be guarded by nine ageless (but not immortal, so that over the centuries many of them lived to see their successors) guardians, who would from then on secretly influence the fate of future generations and civilisations.
