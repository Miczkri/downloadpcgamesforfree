The Sims 4 is the best simulator of ordinary — or extraordinary — life and has already seen dozens of add-ons and major expansions. One of the most interesting is The Sims 4: Get Famous.

## Furniture and clothes

As with any expansion pack, Get Famous introduces new features to the Sim creator and new interior design elements in build mode. In terms of the former, there is a wide range of modern and fashionable clothing for adult Sims, but the main attraction is the stunning evening gowns for women.

The expansion pack introduces many of these, and we can now see how much we were missing them. You have to wear something to these exclusive parties, right? You could even match your evening gown to the interior design.

'Get Famous' introduces two new furniture styles, and the first one is... awful. I suppose that's the idea, as this ostentatious, gold-laden, baroque-inspired, nouveau riche, style-less décor is reminiscent of artificial, pretentious Hollywood, which the expansion pack strongly references.

The second style, on the other hand, is retro and styled after the 1950s and 1960s. It is pleasantly subdued and interestingly distinctive. It will certainly appeal to many fans of furnishing Sim homes.

## Valley of Stars

You'll need to furnish it if you want to live in the new location introduced by the expansion pack. Del Sol Valley is the Sims' version of Hollywood, the American dream factory, divided into plots for aspiring celebrities and big-name stars, with a communal area containing a disco club, gym and park.

Although the main new career in 'Get Famous' is acting, our Sims can follow other paths too. For example, there is a stylist/make-up artist profession, which works like any other classic job where we simply send our Sim to work. Alternatively, you can become a YouTube influencer and work from home.

This has been implemented in an interesting way: not only do we perform in front of the camera, but we also have to edit the material before posting it and ensure it is distributed properly on social media.

## Be like Marilyn Monroe!

However, the main attraction in 'Get Famous' is an acting career. It's an extensive path, similar to those in the “[Get to Work](https://downloadpcgamesforfree.com/the-sims-4-get-to-work-download-free-for-pc/)” expansion pack. We start with auditions, rehearsals and recording commercials for washing powder before moving on to bigger roles.

Subsequent career steps allow us to develop our character and acquire more and more celebrity-like traits, such as narcissism and arrogance. It's great that you can act in films and be a celebrity here. You can also gain fame that is not necessarily good.

## Reputation and fame

The expansion pack introduced a new system that determines how well-known our Sim is and what they are known for. The more famous they are, the greater the impact their actions have on their reputation. This means you have to be very careful not to do anything foolish, such as staring admiringly at the mirror — a habit that our acting Sims are unfortunately very prone to.

The paths to fame are winding, but fortunately, our Sim can step off the stage undefeated at any time and retire from acting to appear in bank commercials.

The Sims 4: Get Famous is a solid expansion pack with great themed content. If you're interested in acting, celebrities, exclusive parties and the dark side of fame, you should definitely get it.
