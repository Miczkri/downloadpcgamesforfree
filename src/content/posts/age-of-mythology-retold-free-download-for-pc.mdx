## Game mechanics

Age of Mythology: Retold is a real-time strategy game in which gameplay is based on managing units (civilian and military), gathering resources, and constructing buildings. It is also necessary to research new technologies and train armies. In addition to economic or military levels, a pantheon of gods, different for each nation, plays a key role in the game (we can choose from Greek, Egyptian, Norse and Atlantean civilizations).

## Calling the gods to battle

By choosing a specific creed, we gain access to useful offensive spells (e.g. earthquake, lightning strike, summoning the dragon Nidhögg) or supportive spells (including summoning rain to fertilize the soil and help the driads). We can also add units from myths (Cyclops, Pegasus, Medusa or Cerberus) to the army, as well as lesser deities whose abilities can decide the fate of many a battle.

The remake offers an extensive campaign consisting of fifty missions. As the story unfolds, we travel through successive ancient lands, taking part in the siege of Troy, fighting battles in the wastelands of Midgard, and unraveling the mysteries of Egypt.

## Game Modes

In addition to the campaign and single-player challenges, the title also features multiplayer play. In Age of Mythology: Retold features a network mode for up to twelve players and the Arena of the Gods, a series of trials that can be completed in cooperation with other fans. The replayability of the scenarios is ensured by procedurally generated maps, and thanks to the built-in editor, we will develop new places for waging wars ourselves.
