## The plot of the game

The game is set in the distant future. We play as an astronaut sent to an inhospitable alien planet with the mission of making it suitable for human colonisation.

Our priority is to survive and then carry out terraforming processes that will warm the planet and create an atmosphere with enough oxygen for the colonists.

## Game mechanics

The Planet Crafter is a survival game in which events are observed from a first-person perspective. The core of the game is the crafting system. The player must collect raw materials and use them to construct new items, machines and a base. There is an extensive technology tree within which all items can be upgraded to increase their usefulness.

Initially, our efforts are focused on survival. The planet has an inhospitable environment that is completely unsuitable for human life. The player must avoid natural hazards and keep an eye on the suit's oxygen supply. Our mission is not the first, so the surface of the alien world is full of shipwrecks that hide useful items.

Once settled on the alien planet, we can begin the process of terraforming it. Using modified plants and futuristic technologies, it is possible to create an atmosphere with oxygen, change the atmospheric pressure, raise the ambient temperature and extract water. This opens up new possibilities for expanding the base and provides access to further improvements. It also introduces new threats, such as changing weather conditions.
