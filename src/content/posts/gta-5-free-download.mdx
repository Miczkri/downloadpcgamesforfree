## Storyline

The action of GTA 5is set in the big open city of Los Santos, where three unusual characters lead a dangerous life. Covered by the witness protection program, ex-robber Michael leads a quiet life until the day he catches his wife cheating on him with a tennis instructor. In anger, he destroys his lover's villa, but it turns out that it was owned by one of the drug barons.

Michael has no choice but to return to his former trade, robbing a jewelry store. However, the burglary draws the attention of his former partner, Trevor Philips, who finds him and persuades him to work together again. The two heroes are joined by a ghetto boy, Franklin, eager to make a large fortune quickly.

## Multiplayer

Grand Theft Auto V is divided into two separate entities - single and multiplayer. In the first case, we take part in the aforementioned story campaign, roam the city in search of finds and secrets, perform side missions and so on. The multiplayer mode, on the other hand, called GTA Online, is devoid of a top-down story and allows for unfettered sandbox gameplay, in which we can become a mafia boss, take part in races, heists, a motorcycle-like mode from Tron and more.
