## The plot of the game

The plot of STALKER 2 once again takes players into the dark areas of the Zona - the vast area surrounding the Chernobyl nuclear power plant, whose accident in 1986 resulted in one of the biggest environmental disasters in history. However, the Ukrainian studio has decided to tweak the true look of the awe-inspiring and fascinating Zone, enriching its territory with warring factions, murderous mutants and deadly anomalies.
