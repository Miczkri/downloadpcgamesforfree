## The plot of the game

The Thaumaturge takes us on a journey to Warsaw in 1905. It is a multicultural metropolis, where under the noses of the upper class, which organizes sumptuous balls, criminal hijinks take place in the dark streets. Located in the Russian Tsar's sphere of influence, the city is a true melting pot, populated by Polish townspeople, Jewish merchants, and Russian soldiers, among others.

Warsaw is also the terrain for the activities of the titular Taumaturgists - people gifted with supernatural abilities who can perceive esoteric beings called Salutors and use them for their needs. One of these taumaturgists is the main character of the described production, Wiktor Szulski, who comes to Warsaw to settle family matters. The protagonist uses his skills to discover the secrets of the city, as well as the secrets hidden by its inhabitants.

It is worth mentioning that although the story told here does not lack fictional elements, it is enriched by the presence of real historical figures.
