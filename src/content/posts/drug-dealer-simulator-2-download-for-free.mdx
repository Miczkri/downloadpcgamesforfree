## Story of the game

Drug Dealer Simulator 2 takes place in the first decade of the 21st century. The main character of the game is Eddie, who goes through a kind of path from zero to (anti)hero. After the protagonist came into conflict with the law, there was nothing left for him but to flee to the island of Isla Sombra. There, Eddie wants to realize his dream of making a real fortune. Step by step, he becomes a real baron and shakes up the criminal underworld of the island.

## How does Drug Dealer Simulator 2 work?

In Drug Dealer Simulator 2 we see the action from a first-person perspective (FPP). The production puts at our disposal a vast open world consisting of numerous towns and villages, as well as picturesque beaches and bays.

We begin our adventure in drug trafficking by buying suspicious substances of various types from under the dark star and selling them for a profit on the street. Over time, we acquire more customers and suppliers, and gain access to better quality drugs. As a result, we reach a point where we have to take care of the purification, mixing, and production of the substances ourselves; this is the only way to achieve a satisfactory quality of the goods we sell.

## Make money and grow your business

If we do everything right, more and more money will find its way into our wallets (interestingly, Isla Sombra's economy is based on two currencies - the local currency and the coveted US dollar). We should invest the accumulated funds primarily in business development, buying new hideouts and laboratories, and improving the ones we already have.

As the scope of our business expands to include more villages and towns, it will become necessary to hire employees. In carrying out the tasks assigned to them, our subordinates take on some of our responsibilities. In exchange for their diligent performance, we can give them ranks, which brings mutual benefits; they have the feeling of climbing the ranks of the criminal career, and we create a gang loyal to us.

## Watch out for enemies

But that's not all, because our activities do not escape the attention of corrupt police, hostile gangs or local militias. We can deal with our enemies by bribing them, by trying to go undercover and sneak up on them, or by making an escape when things get out of hand (for example, during one of the militia's regular raids). The chase scenes are enhanced by the mobility of the protagonist, who skillfully overcomes obstacles in the terrain and jumps over rooftops.

Drug Dealer Simulator 2 has a graphic design of decent quality, but it does not stand out from other unusual simulators.
