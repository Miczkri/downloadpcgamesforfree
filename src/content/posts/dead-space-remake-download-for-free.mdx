## Plot of the game

Engineer Isaac Clarke is one of the crew members of the shuttle USG Kellion, which is sent to the flagship mining ship USG Ishimura to make necessary repairs. A standard mission quickly turns into a desperate fight for survival when it is discovered that the ship's employees have been murdered and bloodthirsty creatures called Necromorphs are roaming the deck. Isaac tries to find out what's behind the horror that has befallen the USS Ishimura while searching for his beloved, medical officer Nicole Brennan, who is stationed on the ship.

The producers of EA Motive made sure that all the storylines of the original Dead Space are included in the remake. They expanded their context with elements known from the second and third parts of the series and comic books set in the same universe.

## Game Mechanics

Dead Space is an action-oriented third-person survival horror game. Embedding a technical worker in the role of the main character meant that in the early stages we do not have firearms at our disposal. However, Isaac Clarke is equipped with tools that turn out to be no less deadly in combat. With their help, the protagonist can lift objects from a distance, slow down enemies or cut off their limbs. The protagonist's accessories also come in handy when solving environmental puzzles.

Isaac also has a professional RIG suit with a display that shows the engineer's current health status. It allows him to work in extremely harsh conditions.
