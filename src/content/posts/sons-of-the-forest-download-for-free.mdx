## The plot of the game

In Sons of the Forest we play the role of a castaway, whose journey aboard a helicopter in the middle of a boundless forest ended in disaster. Our task is to survive and solve the mystery of the island. However, this will not be easy as the area is overrun by mutated creatures and cannibals.

## Game Mechanics

In Sons of the Forest we observe the action from a first-person perspective (FPP). The production provides us with an open world in which we can move freely. In addition to the dense forest, a river with picturesque waterfalls or a vast beach, there are caves bathed in darkness, waiting to be plundered by the brave. Since living here is no idyll, survival aspects and an extensive crafting system play an important role.

If you want to get food, you must first gather wood from which you can make a makeshift weapon. The raw materials, which we have in abundance around us, are also used to build a real base camp (we are talking about a multi-story structure surrounded by a palisade; the tarpaulin stretched on poles can only serve as a roof over our heads for the time being).

As for weapons, a simple spear or even a knife will be enough to kill non-aggressive animals (such as turtles), but to defend ourselves against mutants, not to mention venturing deep into their territories, we will need murderous tools of a much larger caliber.

First and foremost, we're talking about the firearms that come into our hands over time. But even with a stun gun or a powerful Gifle, be careful, because mutants rarely attack alone.
