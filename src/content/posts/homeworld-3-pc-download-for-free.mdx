## Game mechanics

In Homeworld 3, you take control of a fleet of interstellar ships and lead them into conflict with enemy forces. Skirmishes take place in three-dimensional environments, but we rarely have to deal with empty interstellar space. More often than not, we find ourselves fighting amidst giant megaliths, the remnants of an ancient civilisation. The presence of these wrecks can be used to your advantage by using them as shields for your units, or by setting traps for your opponents among them.

In addition, we should take into account the specifics of our fleet when developing tactics for subsequent clashes. Among the ships at our disposal we will find both powerful cruisers of the line and smaller but more agile fighters.
