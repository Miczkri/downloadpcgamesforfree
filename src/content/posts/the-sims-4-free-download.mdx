## Game mechanics

The Sims 4 avoids revolutionary changes to the gameplay model. Throughout the game, the player takes care of the family of virtual Sims, looking after all their spiritual and material needs, providing a roof over their heads, designing a house and a garden.

All gameplay begins with the creation of your Sims. In The Sims 4, it was decided to redesign the character creation system. This time, the facial modification system has been expanded - the sliders that change a given element have been replaced by mouse movements, while increasing the range of changes. The ability to edit the entire silhouette of the character has also been added. On the other hand, the ability to freely customise hair and clothing colours has been removed, returning to the predefined sets provided by the creators.

These affect the character's subsequent behaviour and are part of the game's improved artificial intelligence system. As a result, Sims behave more naturally and react in a much more varied way to the situations around them. The emotions felt by the virtual heroes are also new. These are influenced not only by their current needs and social interactions, but also by the household items they use and the clothes they wear.
