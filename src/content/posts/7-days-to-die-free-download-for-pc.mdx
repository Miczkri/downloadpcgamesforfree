## Story of the game

The action takes place in the near future, after a nuclear war that began in 2034 led to the almost complete annihilation of human civilization. To make matters worse, a large part of the surviving population has been infected with a virus that kills within seven days and then turns its carriers into zombies. Players take on the role of one of the few remaining healthy people trying to survive in the American county of Navezgane. The name means "monster killer" in the Apache language, and that's what we're all about.

## Game mechanics

7 Days to Die takes place in an open-ended world that is largely made up of voxels. The game allows the destruction of most elements of the environment based on an extensive physics engine that calculates the strength of individual objects. The advantage of the production is a large variety of locations, we visit cities, forests, deserts and mountains, among others. Praiseworthy is the fact that players can enter any building they encounter.

The game world is not only inhabited by zombies - we will often face mutants, while hunger and thirst are also dangerous threats, forcing the player to gather provisions. Survival is facilitated by a crafting system where players collect raw materials and construct new items from them. All of this has been implemented in an intuitive way, so there is no need to search for help in online tutorials. There is also an option to repair and upgrade existing buildings. The gameplay is enriched with RPG elements that allow the hero to develop his skills.

In 7 Days to Die on PC, PS4 and XONE, there was also a story thread. The game is equipped with a system that adapts the subsequent story elements to our actions, which means that we always have something to do. In addition to the single player mode, multiplayer games are available - both cooperative and competitive with other users.

## Fight for survival

The game has implemented a system of changing times of day. During the day, zombies are not so threatening - they are weak and slow. At night, however, they are much more dangerous. The dead do a lot to hunt us - they can climb, dig, destroy elements of the environment (walls, doors), etc. And as if that weren't enough, they become more threatening by the day.

In other words, the building must have a solid structure so that it doesn't collapse. Another thing to consider is the strength of the materials. They will deteriorate over time and you will have to buy new ones.

Our avatar in the game world also has to fight hunger and thirst all the time. Necessary items can be found by traversing the local world, but it should also be remembered that the food we carry with us can easily be sensed by zombies.
