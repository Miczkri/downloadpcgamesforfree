## Game mechanics

In terms of gameplay mechanics, Life By You is similar to The Sims series. We start the game by creating our own family. A special wizard has been prepared for this purpose, in which, among other things, we adjust the appearance of the characters.

Then we introduce the characters we have created into an open world where they can move freely. This means that there will be no loading screens in the game and we will be able to control our characters even in such mundane matters as the way to work.

## Bringing the world to life

An important role in the creation of the world is also offered by tools that allow its detailed editing. The plots on which it is possible to build can be rearranged almost without limit. In addition, we can also define the parameters of the population living in the city. Thus, we can determine such aspects as the average age and the predominant skin color of the local population.

During the game we can also design the interiors of the buildings. We can plan the layout of the rooms and choose the furniture. The developers have prepared for us a wide range of options for customizing objects. So we will choose the color with the help of a special wheel. In addition, we will determine what actions can be performed on a given object.

Of course, we also need to control the actions of our characters. Among other things, we have to take care of their basic needs, such as hunger.
