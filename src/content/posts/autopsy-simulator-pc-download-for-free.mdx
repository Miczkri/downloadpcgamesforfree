## Gameplay

In Autopsy Simulator we play the role of a pathomorphologist named Jack. While dissecting the body of a mysterious woman, the protagonist finds a ring belonging to his long-lost wife. Thus begins the nightmare of the protagonist, who, while trying to work and find out what happened to his wife, must face his own weaknesses and dark forces.

In Autopsy Simulator, the action is viewed from a first-person perspective (FPP). The main focus of the game is performing autopsies. Before playing, be prepared for the fact that the autopsy process is presented in a very realistic way.

Various patients are brought to our table. In order to find out what caused the death of each person, we have to examine their entrails step by step. We use syringes to draw blood, and scalpels, forceps, or even our own hands to cut skin and ribs to extract more organs. On the other hand, if we want to get to the brain, we must first make a suitable hole in the skull.

## Pay attention to details

Looking closely at the various organs, we find different injuries on them, and the protagonist's subsequent observations are automatically recorded in the documents. In this way, we can gradually determine the reason for a person's death. However, in order to be sure that we have correctly identified the cause of death, we should carefully study the case file beforehand.

Between each autopsy, we are busy following and advancing the plot. While moving through the morgue, the protagonist has to face frightening dangers and situations, not knowing which of them are real and which are just a product of his imagination.

## Technical aspects

Autopsy Simulator has a graphic design of very good quality. Particularly noteworthy is the detailed design of the bodies that end up on our table; the organs extracted from them are made with attention to detail.
