A cookie is a small, simple file that is sent along with the pages of this website and stored by your browser on the hard drive of your computer or other device. The information stored in it may be sent back to our servers or to the servers of the relevant third parties during a subsequent visit.

4. What is a web beacon?

A web beacon (or clear GIF) is a small piece of text or an invisible image on a website used to track website traffic. To do this, various data about you are stored using web beacons.
