After earning a degree in Electronics and Telecommunications from the Polytechnic School of New York, I decided to dedicate my life to video games. As a child, I would lose myself in *Valle de los Mineros* and win gold medals in *League of Legends*. Twenty years later, I’m still just as passionate about video games as I was back then. These days, my favorite titles are the Persona games and From Software’s “soulslike” games. I stay away from consoles; the PC holds a special place in my heart. After work, I devote myself to my hobbies: I work as a translator, develop my first game, or spend time watching movies and TV shows (mostly cartoons).
